What is a Mermaid Wedding Gown?

First and foremost, a mermaid-style wedding dress is flattering for any figure. We offer dresses for all body types, including plus-size mermaid wedding dresses. A mermaid wedding gown starts with a fitted bodice and skirt, ending with a dramatic flair. The shape resembles a mermaid, hence the name. The dress can have a smaller sweep train or a larger chapel train to provide a stunning look coming and going.

Enjoy Unbounded Opportunities With a Mermaid Wedding Dress

Mermaid wedding dresses are complemented by various materials. Crisp and sturdy fabrics can be used to sculpt your figure, starting at the neckline and continuing down to the knees, where the flair begins. Crepe offers a little more stretch and comfort than materials like satin. With lighter fabrics like chiffon, you can hold yourself like a queen as the layers of fabric trail behind you. Selecting a mermaid lace wedding dress can add a boho vibe or an air of elegance to your special day. A mermaid wedding gown offers the possibility of endless embellishments to amplify your bridal glow.
